✈️ Aer Lemmings

Guide 100 passengers through the airport to check-in!

How to Play

  • Goal: Guide at least 50 passengers to the check-in gate within 5 minutes
  • Click to start the game and advance through levels
  • Spacebar/Escape: Pause/Resume the game
  • Activate Powers: Click on a passenger with a ⭐ (gold star) to select them, then click near a matching obstacle to use their power!
  • Some passengers have special powers (indicated by a gold star and icon):
Green: Bridge Builder - Can build bridges over gaps
Orange: Door Breaker - Can break through doors
Purple: Security Briber - Can bypass security checkpoints
Blue: Regular passenger

⚠️ Watch out for competitor airlines (Ryan Air, Fly Be, Virgin) - they'll stop your passengers!
